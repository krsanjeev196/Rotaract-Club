The purpose of Rotaract is to provide an opportunity for young men and women to enhance the knowledge and skills that will assist them in personal development, to address the physical and social needs of their communities, and to promote better relations between all people worldwide through a framework of friendship and service.


EVENTS

Water To Wings

For our feathered companions, with a vision to diminish the cruelty of the hot summer days. This event is done to give water to the feathered creatures during summer when the temperature is very high. The water bodies which are generally loaded up with over time diminish and flying creatures battle to discover water in their surroundings. Therefore, this initiative attempts to fulfill their need of water by putting earth pots filled with it at various locations.

NGO Visit

We believe there is nothing more beautiful than someone going out ot their way to make life beautiful to others. NGO visits conducted annually brings out that appealing version in every student. That illimitable exchange of love, care, friendship among the students and golden agers is the best to look forward to.
Each year, a large number of students make their way to spend a glorious day in the month of August and interact with the elderly sharing wisdom and learning from experiences.

Career Counselling

"It's not what you achieve, it's what you want to achieve."
Career counselling, an event organised by RCM that provides proper guidance to high school students to make career, educational, and life decisions. It is essential to know the importance of stream that the student is choosing.
Rotaract volunteers visit preselected government high schools to guide the students of class 10th and 12th and provide information of various career options available to them. We assist them int analyzing the strength, interest and weakness in a particular carrier. In addition, we make them aware about various state and central level examinations. Event was conducted in the month of February this year The school selected this year was the Governmebt Higher Secondary School kotra Nehru Nagar.

Collection Drive

"We make a living by what we get, but we make a life by what we give"
It ia a biannual event of Rotaract Club MANIT. We select such localities like Surendra Palace or Aakriti Gardens, where the residents can provide us reusable items like clothes, books, toys, shoes and many other things that can be useful to needy families. We do promote in the selected localities to spread the awareness of the drive within a week before. Our volunteers split up and visit every house of the society to collect the items. At last, we load all the collected items in a vehicle and bring them to the college. Finally, we sort them according to cateogories like children, men, women and miscellaneous (may contain bedsheeets, blankets, sweaters, etc) and pack everything in bags for easy shifting distribution.

Distribution Drive

"A good deed of help is only accomplished when it reaches the right person."
Distribution drive is organized twice a year following the collection drive. In this drive a proper locality is chosen where there are ample number of people in need. Clothes, utennsils, books and other reusable items are distributed among the needy. every year, through this drive essentials are distributed in different places like Nehru Nagar Slums.
